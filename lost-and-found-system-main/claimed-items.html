Report a Discrepancy

If you believe this item was resolved in error, please follow the steps below to contact an administrator for review.

What to do next:

  1. Click the "Copy Report Info" button above.
  2. Go to the Contact Admin page.
  3. Fill in your name and email, and use a clear subject like "Discrepancy Report".
  4. Paste the copied information into the message box.
  5. **Important:** At the top of the message box, describe why you are reporting a discrepancy (e.g., "This is my item but I did not claim it," or "This item was marked resolved by mistake.").
  6. Submit the contact form. An admin will review your report.
